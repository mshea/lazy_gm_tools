#### Potion of Resistance

*Potion, Uncommon*

When you drink this potion, you have Resistance to one type of damage for 1 hour. The GM chooses the type or determines it randomly by rolling on the following table.

| 1d10 | Damage Type |
|------|-------------|
| 1    | Acid        |
| 2    | Cold        |
| 3    | Fire        |
| 4    | Force       |
| 5    | Lightning   |
| 6    | Necrotic    |
| 7    | Poison      |
| 8    | Psychic     |
| 9    | Radiant     |
| 10   | Thunder     |
