Darkvision

Level 2 Transmutation (Druid, Ranger, Sorcerer, Wizard)

Casting Time: Action

Range: Touch

Components: V, S, M (a dried carrot)

Duration: 8 hours

For the duration, a willing creature you touch has Darkvision with a range of 150 feet.
